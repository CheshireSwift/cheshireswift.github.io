Setting Background

  • Tricksters choose a purpose when they are born and are beholden to it. This purpose is always some form of disruption, trouble or harm. It can be targeted at an individual, a group, or the world at large, but it must make someone's life harder. Beyond their specific purpose, all tricksters are comforted by being unhelpful, obstructive and contrary whenever possible.

  • Figaro is a trickster. His chosen purpose is to disrupt the plans of BIG_BAD, which is why he helps the crew and guides the players towards the end of the story.
