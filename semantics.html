What is Semantic HTML?

Semantic HTML is the use of HTML markup to reinforce the semantics, or meaning, of the information in webpages and web applications rather than merely to define its presentation or look. - Wikipedia

Why do we care?

  • Accessibility

Good news!

Headings h1-h6 are one of the most important pieces of semantic information in a page.

(Hopefully you're already using them.)

Going beyond headings

  • "Can I be more precise?"
  • Spectrum of semantics:
    • div section form
    • span code button
  • Starting points:
    • Page regions: main, nav, header/footer
    • Content structure: article, section, aside, p
    • Text: em, strong, code, mark
